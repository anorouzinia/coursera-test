Our Menu

Our exceptional burritos come with three options of meat: Chicken, steak, and shrimp. They come with a side of sourcream or guacamole, black beans, lettuce and tomato, onions, and green peppers. Burritos

We offer several drinks, both alcoholic and non-alcoholic. For cocktails, we have our famous mojitos, margeritas, cherry vodkas, and piña coladas. Our non-alcoholic beverages range from sodas to fresh juices and even milkshakes. Drinks

Of course, our desserts are to die for. With selections ranging from fried ice cream to halwa to crême brulées, our restaurant has no cuisine restrictions. Of course, all desserts will come with a complementary hot chocolate or tea upon demand. Desserts
